Log listeners
====================

KissLog comes with several built-in log listeners:

- :doc:`KissLogApiListener` - saves the logs to KissLog.net

- :doc:`LocalTextFileListener` - saves the logs on local text files

- :doc:`NLogTargetListener` - sends all the logs to **NLog.config** targets
